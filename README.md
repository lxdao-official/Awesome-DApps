## üöÄ Why this list?

In the Web3 ecosystem, open-source DApps are critical infrastructure‚Äîfrom wallets and protocols to creative tools and governance experiments. However, many of the most exciting projects get buried in technical noise or are hard to discover.

This repo aims to highlight the most interesting, creative, and mission-aligned DApps‚Äîespecially those with:

- Unique use cases or social impact
- Innovative onchain mechanics
- Strong ethos of decentralization and openness

With this list, you can:

- Discover new projects (best ones)
- Learn about the latest trends
- Get inspired by the most creative and impactful DApps
- Contribute to the list and help others discover these projects

and most importantly, find the beauty of the world computer!
